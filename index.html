Common questions

Everything you're wondering about.

Quick, honest answers — no fine print, no runaround.

Will filing a hail claim raise my insurance rates?
Hail damage falls under the comprehensive portion of your auto policy — not collision. Comprehensive claims are considered acts of nature, not driver fault, so most carriers do not raise your individual rate for filing one. Always confirm with your specific carrier, but in our experience filing a hail claim is one of the safest claims you can make.
How long does a hail repair take?
Most paintless dent repairs are completed in 3 to 10 business days, depending on damage severity and parts availability. We'll give you an exact timeline at your free inspection — and a complimentary rental can keep you moving while we work.
Do I really pay nothing out of pocket?
That's right. We cover your deductible. Your only contribution is the time it takes to drop off and pick up your vehicle. No hidden fees, no surprise add-ons. The total out-of-pocket cost to you is $0.
What if I've already filed a claim?
Even better — bring us your claim number and the adjuster's contact info, and we'll take it from there. We work directly with your insurance company to complete the process, and you'll still get the same deductible coverage and full-service treatment.
What insurance companies do you work with?
All of them. We have direct experience with State Farm, Allstate, GEICO, Progressive, USAA, Farmers, Liberty Mutual, Nationwide, Travelers, and every other major carrier serving North Texas.
Do you offer free inspections?
Yes — every inspection is 100% free with zero obligation. We can meet you at your home, your office, or you can come to our shop. If your vehicle has hail damage, we'll show you exactly what we see and explain your options.
